Discrepancy Tracker

Suivi des divergences — nightly gaps between the City’s reported capacity and the raw SMIS data

A running log of discrepancies between the City of Toronto’s official overnight shelter counts and the raw SMIS data published on the Open Data portal, by date.
Author

Miriam Marling

Published

June 4, 2026

This page logs, night by night, the gap between the City of Toronto’s reported overnight capacity and the raw SMIS records published on the Open Data portal. Each row records the City’s official figure, our raw-data audit figure, and the variance between them — so the public can see, on a daily basis, where the system fails to meet the zero-unplaced target on nights reaching −5 °C.

Cette page consigne, nuit après nuit, l’écart entre la capacité déclarée par la Ville de Toronto et les données brutes du SMIS. Chaque ligne indique le nombre officiel de la Ville, le nombre de notre audit, et l’écart entre les deux.
